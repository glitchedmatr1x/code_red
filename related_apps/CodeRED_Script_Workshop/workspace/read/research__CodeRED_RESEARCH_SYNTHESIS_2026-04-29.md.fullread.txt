## Gringo Spawn Lead

Existing gringo research remains the strongest lead for real vehicle spawning:

- `Vehicle_Generator`
- `car_gringo`
- `PlayerCar`
- `CarCrank_gringo`
- `Gen_Vehicle_Brain`
- `trainCar_*_gringo`

The prior pass found vehicle-related roots in `commongringos.wgd` and argued that WSI static prop replacement is not enough. The extracted-root scan reinforces this: vehicle behavior appears in gringo scripts, traffic scripts, templates, and camera/locset/tune files together.

Best current interpretation:

1. WSI/world placement can likely reference a gringo host or placement.
2. Gringo dictionary/script layers define use contexts and behavior.
3. Traffic and mission scripts choose when/where to instantiate behavior.
4. Tune/template/locset/model/nav files make the spawned thing usable and visible.
